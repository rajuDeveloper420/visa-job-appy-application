General Questions

Please answer the following questions as accurately as possible.

1. Do you have a high school diploma/GED or education equivalent?
2. Do you have a valid state-issued driver's license?
3. Are you able and willing to work during day and night shifts, weekends, holidays and mandatory overtime based on operational need?
4. Are you willing and able to work outdoors in all temperatures and weather conditions?
5. Are you willing and able to work in an environment with exposure to high noise levels?
6. Are you able to read, write and speak English?
7. Have you done IELTS?
8. Are you willing and able to perform a variety of physical activities 9 including bending, stooping, climbing and working in tight spaces?
10. Reliability and punctual attendance is an essential function of the position. Are you reliable and punctual?
11. Although the position is part-time, do you have the ability to attend up to 5 - 6 weeks of full-time (40 hours/week) training?
12. Have you applied for the position of Ramp Service Employee and previously been interviewed in the last 6 months?
